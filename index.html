Just What is URIEL?



Uriel is the name of your new guardian angel that will accompany you in all your journeys. It is danger detection system that ensures your safety during all the thrilling and high intensity activities you may want to engage in. You can now hike, ski, rock climb, surf, and do numerous other activities without having to worry about your safety. Leave it to Uriel and you are good to go. All you have to do is focus on having FUN!

Here are a few of the many features of Uriel:

A state of the art map API to locate you anywhere at any moment.

Danger zone alerts when you approach a recognized danger zone.

Quick SOS promps SOS using your smartphone's built in beacon signal.

Most recent news updates relevant to your location and area of activity.

Numerous travel tips adapted to your location and choice of activity.
